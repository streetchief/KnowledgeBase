## What is Spicy Pepl?

This Slack was founded by an alumn, but at some point he added David, Nimit, and other FS staffers as admins. Then a few months ago, FS said they were going to shut the space down, _even though they did not own it_, so that everyone would have to use their beloved Fullstack Together <img src="https://emoji.slack-edge.com/T0LN8FJEP/party-poo/3a79531527789c34.gif" alt=":party-poo:" height="20">. So <img src="https://emoji.slack-edge.com/T0LN8FJEP/super/8cf9bf520de08c3f.png" alt=":super:" height="20"> [Sam](https://github.com/samuelleavin) <img src="https://emoji.slack-edge.com/T0LN8FJEP/super/8cf9bf520de08c3f.png" alt=":super:" height="20"> got the keys from the founder of the space (who was no longer active here) and booted the meanies off :put_litter_in_its_place:. (n.b. this occured on 2020-12-03) And then Tessa graciously agreed to co-own the space :crown:, and we have lived in peace and harmony ever since. <img src="https://a.slack-edge.com/production-standard-emoji-assets/13.0/apple-medium/1f54a-fe0f@2x.png" alt=":dove_of_peace:" height="20">

This space used to be called “Fullstack Alumni” or something boring like that, but Sam changed it to Spicy Pepl so that Zovio couldn’t be a pain in the butt about trademark infringement, and nobody’s come up with a good reason for it not to be called Spicy Pepl henceforth.

-- [@indiamos](https://www.indiaamos.com/)
