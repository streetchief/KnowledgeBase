# Interviewing (Being Interviewed)

## General

- Be specific, not arrogant.
- Limit details.
- Focus on yourself, not the team.
- S.T.A.R. - Situation, Task, Action, Result.

## Responding

### S.T.A.R. Method

1. Situation
2. Task
3. Action
4. Result

### Cracking the Coding Interview

1. Story summary
2. Nugget
3. Situation
4. Actions
5. Result
6. What it says about me

### S.O.A.R. Method

1.  Situation
2.  Obstacles
3.  Actions
4.  Results
