# money market security

## Definition
Short-term security, highly liquid.

## Notes
- They are usually sold in large denominations.
- They have low default risk.
- They mature in one year or less from their original issue date. Most money market instruments mature in less than 120 days.
- Most money market securities do not pay interest - they are discounted.
- An active secondary market makes it easy to find buyers in the future.
